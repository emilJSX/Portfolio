About Myself

I'm Aysel Rzayeva, am currently 19 years old and I'm studying at Azerbaijan Architeccture and Construction University (AACU). It was always my goal to study architecture. Considering the fact that people have always been surrounded by architecture, it has always played a big role in their lives. Architecture is a visual representation of development of each nation and its own needs. The starting point of my interest has been my grandfather, who was the member of Union of Architects in the UdSSR. He was a guide for me in the period of my childhood, who gave me inspiration to turn my hobby into my passion. For me personally this is not just a type of a profession at the moment. It's a form of self interpretation and a form of art. Since I'm already studying this field, half of my dream already became true. But I understand that this is only just a beginning of my journey and I know that I have to challenge and improve myself all the time in order to reach my goal. Therefore I'm seeking for better opportunities of academic studies. During the years 2015/16, I was an exchange student in Germany (Hamburg). I had an opportunity to get to know the culture, see the architecture and make friends. These were the factors that led to my decision of starting my Bachelors degree in Germany. I really hope that I will be able to achieve my goal, become a good professional in this field and prove those people wrong, who think that architecture isn't a proper profession for women.
