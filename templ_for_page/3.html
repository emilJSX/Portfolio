General Plan

On the picture you can see the General Plan of the chosen area.(Which is divided in zones as well)

There are all necessary zones included to the plan: starting from parking, ending up with outdoor reading zones. The parking itself is a very important part of this whole plan and you can reach the library from 3 directions. The road that leads to the building will let the trucks drive by and unload all of the loads to the storage located on the right side of the building.

The plan includes 3 lakes to its site, that are called "Trinity Lakes". These water elements reduce the sound spread and besides, people can simply relax and spend time around them.

Summer Theater is going to be a zone where students and professors or simply any person could make speechs or hold events.

The main idea behind this plan was to have some angled shapes around, but show them in a more smooth manner, providing the site with lots of green space and open area at the same time.
